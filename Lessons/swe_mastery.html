Prompt Engineering Documentation

Core Prompting Architecture

Zero-shot: Providing instructions without any examples.

Few-shot: Guiding behavior via specific examples provided in the prompt.

Role Prompting: Defining the AI's behavior, tone, and expertise level.

Chain-of-Thought: Forcing multi-step reasoning to improve output quality.

Safety & Integration

Guardrails: Constraints designed to maintain safety and reduce harmful outputs.

Structured Outputs (JSON): Crucial for parsing, automation, and system integration.

State Management: Tracking conversation progress for multi-turn interactions.
